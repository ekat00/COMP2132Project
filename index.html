GAME RULES

It's you, SCORPION, against your mortal enemy SUB-ZERO!

The rules are simple:

  • Each game consists of 3 dice rolls
  • The sum of the dice is your current score
  • The sum of the rolls is your total score
  • If you roll a double, you get double points! E.g. rolling two 3s gives you 12 points ((3+3)*2)
  • If you roll a 1, you get zero (0) points for that roll
  • The player with the most points after 3 rolls is the winner!
